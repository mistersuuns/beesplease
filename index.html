About Bees Please

Bees Please is a Cornwall-based sister company of Higgledy Garden, offering bee friendly flower seed packets for retail, fundraising, charity or promotion purposes. Our seed packets can be printed to your own design or with our BeesPlease branding.

Our eco-friendly seed packets contain a beautiful mix of flower seeds drawn from a range of species including Borage, Calendula, Cornflower, Cosmos, Dill mammoth, Eschscholtzia, Nigella and Phacelia.

Other flowers can be included on request.
